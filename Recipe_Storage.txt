Stuffed Mushrooms
9
1 lb mushrooms
4 slices of bacon
2 T minced green pepper
1/4 cup minced onion
1/2 tsp salt
3 oz cream cheese
1/4 tsp. worcestershire sauce
1T butter
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
Clean mushrooms and remove stem. Cook bacon and crumble.  Melt butter in pan and cook onions and green pepper.  Add remaining ingredients, except breadcrumbs. Include chopped mushroom stems. Fill mushrooms with mixture and top with breadcrumbs. Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 15 min or until breadcrumbs are browned.
Panko Crusted Chicken Drumsticks
13
6 chicken drumsticks
1 egg
1T water
4T butter
3/4 cup panko
2T parmesian cheese
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp paprika
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp dried oregano
1/4 tsp dried basil
1/4 tsp pepper
Preheat oven to 425. Melt butter in cast iron skillet. Whisk together egg and water. Combine breadcrumbs, parmesan, salt, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, basil, and pepper. Dip drumsticks in egg then crumbs. Place in pan. Bake for 30 min, flip, bake 15 min more.
Mac and Cheese
7
4 cups elbow macaroni
2 cups grated cheddar
3 eggs beaten
1/2 cup sour cream
4 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup milk
Preheat oven to 350 deg, cook macaroni. While still hot, add the cheddar. In a seperate bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Add macaroni. Put in cassarole dish and cook 30-45 min.
Beef Broccoli Stir Fry
15
2 3.5 oz bags of rice
2 tbls dry sherry, divided
2 tbls soy sauce, divided
1 tsp sugar
1 lb boneless sirloin steaks
1/2 cup beef broth
1 tbls cornstarch
1 tbls hoisin sauce
1 tsp sriracha or 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
2 tbls canola oil, divided
1 tbls bottled fresh ginger
2 tsp minced garlic
4 cups prechopped broccoli
1/4 cup water
1/3 cup sliced green onions
1) cook rice 2) combine 1 tbls sherry, 1 tbls soy sauce, sugar and beef. Stir together 1 tbls sherry, 1 tbls soy sauce, broth, corn starch, hoison, and sriracha. 3) Heat 1 tbls oil in wok over medium heat. Add beef mixture; saute 3 min or until browned. Remove beef. Add remaining 1 tbls oil to pan. Add ginger and garlic; cook 30 sec, stirring constantly. Add brocolli and 1/4 cup water, cook 1 min. Add onions, cook 1 min, stirring constaly.Add broth mixture and beef mixture; cook 2 min or until beef is thoroughly heated and sauce is slightly thick. Serve beef over rice.
Cheesy Tabasco Potatoes
9
4 russet pototoes, sliced
1/2 cup butter
1 tbl grated onion
1/2tsp salt
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp tabasco
1/4 tsp back pepper
1/2 cup cheese
1 tbl parsley
Place half of the potatoes in a greased pan. Melt butter and mix with onion, salt, oregano, tabasco, and pepper. Cook briefly. Brush potatoes with half the butter mixture. Top with remaining ingredients, then brush with remaining butter mixture. Bake for 45 min at 425 degrees. Top with cheese and parsley and bake an additional 10 min.
Hamburger Helper
15
1T olive oil
1/2 cup onion
1 lb ground beef
1.5 cloves garlic
1/2 tsp dried basil
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
1/8 cup flour
14 oz tomato sauce
1/2 cup beef broth
1/2 lb pasta
1/2 cup pasta
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 cup cheese
Cook olive oil, onion, ground beef.  Add garlic, basil, oregano, salt, crushed red pepper, flour and cook for 1 min on medium. Add tomato sauce, beef broth.  Boil and simmer 15 min.  Add pasta, heavy cream. Add cheese, stir until melted.
